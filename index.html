Built for Every
Type of Clinic

Whether you're a solo GP or a multi-specialty chain, sambandh.ai handles the entire patient journey — from first visit to loyal advocate.

📱

QR Patient Capture

One QR at reception. Every new patient scans, opens WhatsApp, and joins your database instantly — name, phone, and visit reason saved automatically.

💬

WhatsApp Follow-ups

Auto-send appointment reminders, test result alerts, medication schedules, and post-visit care tips. Reduce no-shows by up to 40%.

📅

Smart Scheduling

Let patients book via WhatsApp. Auto-confirm, send 24-hour reminders, and intelligently manage your daily appointment load.

Google Review Engine

After every visit, auto-send review requests. Happy patients leave stars; unhappy ones message you privately — protecting your online reputation.

Patient Segmentation

Auto-group patients into Chronic (3+ visits), New, Lapsed, and VIP. Target each group with hyper-relevant health tips and recall campaigns.

📊

Revenue Analytics

Track per-doctor, per-treatment, and per-branch revenue. Understand peak hours and predict next month's income with confidence.
